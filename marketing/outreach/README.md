# Dated — Business Outreach Templates

Email templates for reaching out to local Vancouver businesses about advertising and partnership opportunities on Dated.

---

## How Dated Monetizes for Partners

Dated offers three tiers of business placement:

**Tier 1 — Featured Listing (Free / Entry)**
Your venue appears prominently in relevant category feeds. No cost — designed to build inventory and relationships.

**Tier 2 — Sponsored Placement ($)**
Priority placement in category feeds, the "Choose a date for me" randomizer, and search results for relevant date types. Priced per month or per click (TBD).

**Tier 3 — Lead Gen / Booking Affiliate (Revenue share)**
Couples click through to book, reserve, or buy directly from your listing. We earn a referral fee per conversion. Similar to OpenTable's model.

## General Outreach Tips

- **Personalize the first line** — always reference something specific about their business
- **Lead with value** — what's in it for them, not what Dated is
- **Keep it short** — under 200 words for cold email, under 100 words for cold DM
- **One clear CTA** — a call, a reply, a visit to the site. Not all three.
- **Follow up once** — 5–7 days after first email if no response
- **Best time to send** — Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am
