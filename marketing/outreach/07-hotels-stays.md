## Customization Notes

- **Tofino properties**: lead with the experience — ocean, wild coast, no cell service. Couples go there for a reason.
- **Gulf Islands B&Bs**: intimate, artsy, couples-only vibe already. Easy pitch — they're already the target audience.
- **Whistler hotels**: ski season + summer season, Day Trip and Vacation year-round
- **Okanagan wineries with stays**: crossover with the winery template — full stay + wine + food = premium date package
- **Glamping operators**: Experiences + Vacation overlap, very Instagram-friendly, high engagement reviews
- **Booking affiliate model is most valuable here**: hotel stays are high-ticket, good referral fee potential
- Always mention the direct booking link potential — accommodation has the clearest conversion path
