## What "Choose a Date for Me" Is

When a couple taps "Choose a date for me" on Dated, they tell the app their:
- Budget (e.g. under $50, $50–$150, $150+)
- Vibe (romantic, adventurous, casual, etc.)
- Location (neighbourhood, city)
- Type (restaurant, activity, outdoor, etc.)

The app then recommends a full date night — venue, plan, booking links.

Sponsored placement means a partner's business is prioritised in those recommendations for matching criteria. The couple still sees something genuinely relevant — we don't recommend a fine dining restaurant to someone who said "budget under $50."

This is the highest-value placement on Dated because the couple has explicitly asked for a recommendation. They're ready to go.

## Partner Pitch Deck Talking Points

Use these when presenting in person or on a call:

**The opportunity:**
- X couples use Dated in Vancouver every month (update with real numbers)
- "Choose a date for me" is the highest-intent surface in the product
- Couples who use it are ready to act — they've already decided to go on a date
- You only appear when genuinely relevant — criteria-matched placement

**The format:**
- Couple selects: budget / vibe / location / type
- App returns: full date plan with your venue as the anchor
- Couple clicks through to your website, booking page, or reservation link
- Tracked: impressions, clicks, conversions (if booking link is provided)

**Pricing model (proposed):**
- Monthly flat fee for guaranteed category placement: starting at $X/month
- Per-click model for performance-based advertisers: $X per click
- Revenue share for booking link conversions: X% per completed reservation/booking

**Why now:**
- Early partners get preferential rates before platform scales
- Vancouver-first means no national noise — every couple seeing your placement is local
- Category exclusivity available for launch partners (e.g. only one fine dining restaurant per neighbourhood per month)

## Customisation Notes

- This template works for ANY business type — use it as the upgrade pitch once you have a relationship from templates 01–09
- The "criteria-matched" angle is the key differentiator vs. Google Ads or Instagram ads — hammer it
- Category exclusivity is a powerful lever for closing early partners — use it carefully
- For high-ticket businesses (hotels, guided tours, fine dining): performance/affiliate model is best
- For lower-ticket businesses (cafes, casual restaurants): flat monthly rate is simpler to sell
