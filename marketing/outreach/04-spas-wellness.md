## Customization Notes

- Lead with **anniversary and special occasion** — wellness sells best as "treat yourself" not "regular Tuesday"
- Float centres: emphasize that it's unusual and memorable — which is exactly what makes a date review worth writing
- Yoga/fitness studios with couples classes: lighter placement, focus on "active wellness date" angle
- High-end spas: exclusivity angle — "we're selective about our wellness partners"
- For venues offering gift cards: push the lead gen/affiliate model — couples buy gift cards as anniversary presents, high conversion
