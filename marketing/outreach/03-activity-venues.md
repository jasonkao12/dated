## Customization Notes

- Escape rooms: lean into "you'll remember this date" — experience reviews are the most detailed and emotional
- Pottery/painting: strong Instagram tie-in — couples post photos, which drives awareness for the venue too
- Cooking classes: high-end angle, anniversary and romantic categories apply
- Axe throwing / bowling: Games & Entertainment category, lean into the "fun and different" angle
- For venues with online booking: push the affiliate/lead gen model harder — they already have conversion infrastructure
