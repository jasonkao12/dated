## Customization Notes

- Check if the venue already has reviews on Dated before sending — reference specific reviews or ratings if so
- For high-end venues (Kissa Tanto, St. Lawrence, Miku tier): lead with exclusivity — "we're keeping this selective"
- For neighbourhood gems: lead with local love — "we're built for Vancouver couples, not tourists"
- For new restaurants: offer a free featured listing to help them build early reviews
