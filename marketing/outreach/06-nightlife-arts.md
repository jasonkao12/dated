## Customization Notes

- **Cocktail bars**: ambiance is the selling point — lean into it hard. Couples spend more at bars when it's a date than any other context.
- **Live music venues**: ticketing angle is strong — direct booking link is high value for both sides
- **Comedy clubs**: opening night / slower nights are where featured placement adds the most value — fill seats
- **Galleries**: Arts & Culture angle, opening events and tours work as date nights
- **Film festivals / one-off events**: time-sensitive, push urgency — "couples need to find this before it sells out"
- **Nightlife on weeknights**: featured placement helps drive weeknight covers, not just peak Friday/Saturday
