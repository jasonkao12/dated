## Customization Notes

- **Florists**: the "add to your date" concept is compelling — like adding a bouquet to an OpenTable reservation
- **Chocolatiers**: local angle is key, especially around Valentine's Day and anniversaries
- **Experience boxes**: affiliate model is the strongest pitch here — clear conversion, trackable
- **Jewellers**: high-value, low-volume play. One proposal referral is worth a lot. Be respectful of tone — this is a big moment.
- **Candle / home goods makers**: Romantic category, not just Anniversary — date nights at home are a real category
- **Gift card angle**: any business with gift cards can participate in a "gift a date" feature
