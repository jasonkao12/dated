## Customization Notes

- **Kayak/canoe/paddleboard rentals**: highest priority — these are already date destinations, easy conversion
- **Guided tour operators**: push the lead gen model hardest here — they have booking infrastructure and high ticket prices (good referral fees)
- **Whale watching / helicopter tours**: luxury experiences, anniversary angle, high referral fee potential
- **E-bike and cycling tours**: summer dates, day trip category, urban + scenic
- **Rock climbing gyms with intro lessons**: Indoor Activities + Experiences crossover — first date and casual vibes
- Seasonality matters — tailor the email to what's relevant right now
