## Customization Notes

- **Okanagan wineries**: Road Trip angle is strongest — couples make a whole weekend of it. Multiple winery partnership is possible: "Dated's Okanagan Wine Route"
- **Fraser Valley**: Day trip angle, closer to Vancouver, more spontaneous
- **Vancouver craft breweries**: Casual date, budget-friendly crossover. Lower placement fee but high volume.
- **Cideries**: growing trend, appeals to the outdoor/nature audience too
- **Distilleries with tours**: Experiences category, unique date, high review quality
- **Wineries with accommodation**: massive — combine with hotel template, full destination partnership
- **Event-based wineries**: time-sensitive, push urgency in the email, propose direct event ticket link
