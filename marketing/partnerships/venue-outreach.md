# Dated — Venue & Partnership Outreach

---

## Why Venues Should Care About Dated

Dated is a review platform built specifically for couples — not tourists, not solo diners, not groups. Every review is from someone on an actual date. That's a high-intent audience for restaurants and experience venues.

**The pitch:** Get featured on Dated and reach couples actively looking for their next date spot.

---

## Partnership Tiers (Proposed)

### Tier 1 — Free: Featured Listing
- Venue appears prominently in the Dated feed for their category
- "Featured" badge on their place page
- We share their listing with our audience via Instagram/TikTok

**What we ask:** Permission to feature them, a photo for the listing, and optionally a comment from the venue for our feed.

### Tier 2 — Sponsored: "Choose a date for me" Placement
- Venue is prioritized in the upcoming "Choose a date for me" randomizer
- Shown to couples looking for that venue type (restaurant, activity, cafe, etc.)
- Tracked clicks and views reported monthly

**Cost:** TBD — start with barter/gifted, move to paid as audience grows

### Tier 3 — Affiliate / Lead Gen (future)
- Booking links through Dated (OpenTable-style)
- We earn a referral fee per reservation driven from the app
- Full analytics dashboard for venue partners

## Gifted Date Night Program

Partner with 2–3 venues to offer UGC creators a gifted dinner in exchange for content featuring both the venue and Dated. Win-win-win:

- Venue gets content and exposure
- Creator gets a free dinner
- Dated gets organic UGC

Structure: We coordinate, venue covers the dinner, creator tags both @getdated and the venue.
