# Dated — UGC Creator Brief

---

## What is Dated?

Dated is a free app where couples rate and review their dates — like Yelp, but for your actual date nights. You log where you went, rate it across 6 categories (ambiance, food, service, value, vibe, overall), add photos, and build a history of every date you've been on.

There's also a **Date Builder** to plan full nights out with multiple stops, a feed of reviews from couples in your city, and an AI tool that suggests date categories based on your review.

It's live now at **getdated.app**. Vancouver-first, expanding across BC.

---

## Who is it for?

Couples who:
- Actually care about their date nights
- Are tired of forgetting where they went / what was good
- Want to find new spots based on real couple reviews (not TripAdvisor tourists)
- Like the idea of having a "date history" they can look back on

1. **It's free** — always mention this

4. **Real reviews from real couples** — not algorithm-driven or sponsored
5. **Date Builder** is a great feature to show — plan the whole night, share a link

---

## What NOT to Say

- Don't call it a "dating app" — it's NOT for finding dates, it's for people already in relationships
- Don't oversell the romance angle — keep it casual and real
- Don't use corporate language ("innovative platform", "cutting-edge")
- Don't promise it'll "save your relationship" or make exaggerated claims
- Don't fake enthusiasm — genuine and conversational always wins

---

## Tone

Casual. Honest. Slightly funny. Like you're telling a friend about something cool you found.

---

## Deliverable Options

Pick ONE of these formats — don't try to cram them all into one video:

**Option A: "I've been rating all my dates"**  
First-person account. Show the app. Talk through a real or example review. End with CTA.  
Length: 30–60 seconds

**Option B: "We did a date night and rated it"**  
Couple POV. Go on a date, film b-roll, open the app at the end and write the review together.  
Length: 45–90 seconds

**Option C: "Vancouver date spots tier list"**  
Use the app to rank Vancouver spots you've actually been to. Or create a tier list based on reviews you see in the app.  
Length: 45–60 seconds

**Option D: "App walkthrough"**  
Screen record the app. Walk through the feed, write a review, show Date Builder.  
Length: 30–45 seconds

---

## Call to Action

End every video with ONE of these:
- "It's free — link in bio"
- "Download Dated at getdated.app"
- "We're using this for every date now — getdated.app"

Don't use multiple CTAs. One, clear, direct.
