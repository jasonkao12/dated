# Dated — UGC Scripts

Three full scripts ready to hand to creators. Each is 30–60 seconds.

---

## Script 1 — "I've been rating all my dates" (Talking Head)

**Best for:** Solo creator, talking to camera, phone visible  
**Length:** ~45 seconds  
**Tone:** Casual, slightly surprised at how useful it is

---

**[HOOK — first 2 seconds, text on screen or spoken]**
*"I've been rating every date I go on like a Yelp review and it's kind of changed everything"*

**[BODY]**
*"So there's this app called Dated — it's free — and basically you review your date nights the same way you'd review a restaurant."*

*"You rate the ambiance, the food, the service, the vibe, the value. You write a little note about how it went. You add photos if you want."*

[hold up phone, show app briefly]

*"And now I have this whole history. Every place we've been, every rating, which spots are worth going back to, which ones weren't."*

[show a specific review on screen]

*"This one — Kissa Tanto — was the best date we've had this year. 5 out of 5 across the board. We're going back for our anniversary."*

**[CTA]**
*"It's called Dated. It's free. We're using it for every date now."*

[text overlay: getdated.app]

## Script 2 — "We did a date night and rated it" (Couple POV)

**Best for:** Couple filming together, b-roll of date night, end with app  
**Length:** ~60 seconds  
**Tone:** Fun, light, genuine

---

**[HOOK — show couple getting ready or arriving at a venue]**
Text overlay: *"Rating our date like a Yelp review 🍷"*

**[AT THE VENUE — b-roll of food, space, atmosphere]**
Voiceover or text overlays:
- "Arrived at Kissa Tanto. Ambiance check." → text: *9/10*
- "Food arrived. Oh wow." → text: *10/10*
- "Service was attentive but not annoying." → text: *9/10*
- "The vibe? Vinyl playing. Low lighting. Perfect." → text: *10/10*

**[END OF NIGHT — show phone with Dated open]**
*"Okay, writing the actual review."*

[brief screen recording of filling in Dated review]

*"Overall: 9.5 out of 5. Would absolutely go back."*

**[CTA — to camera or text overlay]**
*"We're doing this every date now. The app's called Dated — it's free — and if you're in Vancouver there are already a bunch of real reviews on it."*

[text: getdated.app]

## Script 3 — "Problem → Solution" (Talking Head)

**Best for:** Direct, punchy creator style — no fluff  
**Length:** ~35 seconds  
**Tone:** Relatable problem, clean solution

---

**[HOOK]**
*"You remember your favourite restaurant. You don't remember your favourite date."*

**[PROBLEM]**
*"We've been on so many good dates this year and I can barely name three of them. Can't remember where we went, what was good, why we loved it."*

**[SOLUTION — show app]**
*"There's this app called Dated where you review your dates like restaurants. Ambiance, food, service, vibe — all rated, all saved."*

[scroll through feed quickly]

*"It also has a feed of other couples in your city so you can find new spots based on real reviews."*

**[CLOSE]**
*"It's free. It's called Dated. If you're in Vancouver, check it out."*

[text: getdated.app]

## Shooting Tips for All Scripts

- Film vertically (9:16) for TikTok/Reels
- Natural lighting when possible — no ring light look if you can avoid it
- Don't over-edit — 2–3 cuts max, no flashy transitions
- Captions on — most people watch without sound
- The app screen recording should be real (log in and use the actual app)
- B-roll of actual Vancouver locations is gold — use it if you have it
