# Dated — Logo Design Brief

Use this brief with a designer, Fiverr, 99designs, or an AI design tool (Looka, Canva, Adobe Firefly).

---

## What We Need

A logo system for **Dated** — a date review app for couples. Think Letterboxd meets Yelp, built for couples who want to document and improve their date life.

---

## Brand Feel

- Minimal and modern — not cutesy or over-romantic
- Warm and friendly — approachable, not corporate
- Slightly playful — the name "Dated" has a double meaning (past tense + going on a date) which we love
- Inspired by: Letterboxd logo style, Linear.app wordmark, Notion icon simplicity

---

## Typography

**Font:** Nunito Extrabold (Google Fonts — free)  
The wordmark should use Nunito Extrabold. This matches the app's UI font.

## Icon Mark Concept

A **minimalist calendar page** with a **small heart** inside the date square. Think:
- Square with rounded corners (like a calendar app icon)
- A small grid suggesting dates
- One date square highlighted with a heart or filled dot
- No gradients — solid fills only
- Works at 16px and at 1024px

Alternative concept: Just the letter **"D"** styled with a heart subtly integrated — like the counter of the D forms a heart shape.

---

## Don'ts

- Don't use gradients in the icon mark
- Don't stretch or distort the wordmark
- Don't use the purple logo on a purple background
- Don't add drop shadows or glows
- Don't use script / cursive fonts
