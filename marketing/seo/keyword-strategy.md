## Content Strategy

### Blog Post Ideas (build these as `/blog` pages on getdated.app)

1. **"15 Best Date Spots in Vancouver, Ranked by Real Couples"**  
   Pull top-rated places from the Dated database. Update monthly. This should rank for "best date spots Vancouver."

2. **"The Ultimate Vancouver Date Night Guide by Neighbourhood"**  
   Gastown, Kitsalano, Mount Pleasant, Yaletown, North Shore. Each with 3–5 venue picks.

3. **"Budget Date Ideas in Vancouver (Under $50)"**  
   Target people who can't afford Miku every week. Directly tied to the Budget-friendly category.

4. **"Vancouver Day Trip Date Ideas: Sea to Sky, Deep Cove, Whistler"**  
   Targets the day-trip category and longer search intent.

5. **"Best Romantic Restaurants in Vancouver for Anniversaries"**  
   High transactional intent. Include Kissa Tanto, St. Lawrence, Miku, Nightingale.

6. **"First Date Ideas in Vancouver That Actually Work"**  
   Tied to the First Date category. Tons of search volume here.

7. **"Date Night in Vancouver: Seasonal Guide (Spring/Summer/Fall/Winter)"**  
   One page per season. Evergreen + seasonal traffic.

## On-Page SEO Priorities

**Review pages (`/r/[slug]`):**
- Title: `[Review title] — [Venue], [City] | Dated`
- Meta description: Pull first 150 chars of review body
- Schema: Review schema markup (rating, author, venue)

**User profile pages (`/u/[username]`):**
- Title: `[Display name]'s date reviews | Dated`

**Place pages (future):**
- Title: `[Venue name] date night reviews — [City] | Dated`
- Aggregate rating from all reviews of that place

**Home page:**
- Title: `Dated — Date review app for couples | Vancouver`
- Meta: "Rate your dates. Discover the best date spots in Vancouver from real couple reviews."
