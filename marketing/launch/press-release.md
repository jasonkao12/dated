## Vancouver Startup Launches "Dated" — A Date Review App for Couples

*New app lets couples rate, review, and remember every date night — like Yelp, but for your relationship*

**VANCOUVER, BC — April 2026** — A new Vancouver-built app called **Dated** is making it easier for couples to remember, rate, and discover their best date nights.

Available now at **getdated.app**, Dated lets couples review their dates across six categories — ambiance, food, service, value, vibe, and overall — and build a searchable history of every date they've been on. Think Letterboxd for film, or Yelp for restaurants — but designed entirely around the experience of going on a date.

"We kept forgetting where we'd been and what we loved," said Jason Kao, founder of Dated. "You'd be trying to book somewhere special and realize you couldn't remember the name of that incredible restaurant from eight months ago. We built Dated to fix that."

Beyond personal logs, Dated features a public feed of reviews from couples in the same city, a **Date Builder** for planning full multi-stop nights out, and an AI-powered category tool that suggests how to tag a review based on the details.

The app launched with a focus on Vancouver and British Columbia, with featured reviews at local favourites including Kissa Tanto, Miku, Stanley Park, the Sea to Sky Gondola, and Deep Cove.

**Key features:**
- Rate dates across 6 categories
- Write reviews with photos
- Browse a local feed of couple reviews
- Build and share full date plans
- Discover new spots filtered by category (romantic, budget-friendly, first date, outdoor, and more)
- Follow other couples and see their date history

Dated is free to use and available now on the web at getdated.app, with a mobile app available for iOS and Android.
