## First Comment (maker comment — post this yourself within minutes of launch)

```
Hey PH! 👋 Jason here, founder of Dated.

I built this after realizing my partner and I couldn't remember half the places we'd been on dates. You'd want to recommend somewhere or go back for an anniversary and... nothing. Total blank.

Dated is the app I wanted to exist. You review your dates like you'd review a restaurant — rate the ambiance, food, service, vibe, and value. Write a note. Add photos. And over time you build this history of every date you've been on.

There's also:
- A public feed of reviews from couples in your city
- Date Builder — plan a full night out with multiple stops and share a link
- AI category suggestions when you write a review
- Follow other couples, save dates you want to try

We launched Vancouver-first (we're based here) but it works anywhere.

A few things I'd love feedback on:
1. The "Choose a date for me" randomizer — coming soon, want to know what you'd want from it
2. The feed discovery — how do you want to browse? By city? Category? Price?
3. Mobile app experience — Expo/React Native, let me know what feels off

It's free. Sign up at getdated.app.

Happy to answer anything — drop it below 👇
```
