# Dated — Influencer & Creator Outreach

---

## Who to Target

**Tier 1 — Vancouver lifestyle/couples creators (1k–50k followers)**
- Couples who post date nights, restaurants, or Vancouver content
- Food bloggers based in Vancouver
- Local lifestyle creators

Look for creators posting: #vancouverdates #yvr #datenight #vancouvercouple #vancouverfoodie

**Tier 2 — Micro-creators (500–5k followers)**
- High engagement rate matters more than follower count here
- Vancouver-based, active on TikTok or Instagram Reels
- Couples content preferred but not required

**Tier 3 — Relevant adjacent creators**
- Vancouver food reviewers (they already understand review culture)
- Relationship / dating advice creators (not dating app people — relationship wellness)
- Travel + local experience creators

## Gifted Date Night Pitch

For local creators, offer a **gifted date night at a partner Vancouver restaurant** (once you have venue partnerships set up — see `partnerships/venue-outreach.md`).

**What we offer:**
- Dinner for two at a featured Dated venue (e.g. Kissa Tanto, Miku, St. Lawrence)
- Access to the app + featured profile placement
- No mandatory posting — we'd love organic content but won't force it

**What we ask for:**
- One honest TikTok or Reel about the experience
- Mention Dated in the caption
- Use `#dated` and `#getdated`
